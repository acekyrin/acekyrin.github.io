Current Events, Summer 2014

This summer I have the pleasure of interning in the Bureau of Education and Cultural Affairs in the U.S. State Department!


The specific office I'm working in will be the Office of Academic Exchange Programs. It deals with helping international students acclimate to American higher education, so I will be working a lot in student affairs


And two other exciting tidbits of exciting news have occurred in the last week; First, I will be volunteering with the River Phoenix Center for Peace Building . I will be learning conflict mediation skills, as well as techniques in restorative justice.


Secondly, I will be serving on the Student Advisory Board for Population Media, a worldwide organization that strives to promote women's fertility issues through television programming and radio shows.


Every few months, I'll have another update, so be sure to check back!
