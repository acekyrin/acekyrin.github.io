My Radio Work

Getting involved in the campus radio station turned out to be one of the best decisions I had made as a college student. Never before was I equipped with so much technology and influence all at my fingertips.


I began my venture into the world of broadcast journalism by becoming a volunteer at the Radio Reading Service my freshman year. The concept was simple: read newspaper articles on-air, tuned to a specific frequency which blind residents had access to.
